Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this tool free to use?

A: Yes, our base platform is free for students and educators.

Q: Do you support real-time collaboration?

A: Yes! Our system allows students and teachers to collaborate on simulations remotely.

Q: What devices are supported?

A: Any device with a modern browser: laptops, tablets, and smartphones.

Q: Can teachers track student progress?

A: Absolutely! Teachers have access to dashboards that show experiment completion and performance analytics.

Q: Can I use this on my mobile device?

A: Yes, the platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly on phones and tablets.

Q: Are AR/VR devices mandatory?

A: No, AR/VR features are optional. The core platform works on standard devices too.

Q: How often is the platform updated?

A: We release updates monthly with new experiments, bug fixes, and performance enhancements.

Q: Is training provided for educators?

A: Yes, we offer onboarding webinars and video tutorials for teachers and institutions.
